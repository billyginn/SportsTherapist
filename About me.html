About me

My name is Billy Ginn. I am 19 years old, English. I was born in Bedford, Befordshire, and that's where I was raised.

As you probably already know by the introduction on my website - I am a first year Undegraduate student of Sports Therapy at the University of Bedfordshire. I chose this course because it is much more practical than many other courses available, and yet I will still get a bachelor degree at high education from a university. If anyone is showing interest in this course - they can either get in touch with me, or google "Sports Therapy University of Bedfordshire", and get all the relevant information.

My hobbies include football, American football and paintball. I am a very big fan of West Ham United. Why? Because it runs in the family. :)

At university, we do therapy, providing massage every week on different clients, however my first real practice was on the 26 April, 2015. I was working alongside Great Ormand Street Hospital at the London marathon. I was providing runners with post-event therapy, after they had run 26.2 miles. I was working that day for four and a half hours, non-stop. Both the athletes and my supervisors were very satisfied.
